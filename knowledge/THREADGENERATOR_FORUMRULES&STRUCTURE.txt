# **Welcome to the Project Ideas Forum!**  

This forum is for sorting and refining project ideas before they become full projects. Every idea will go through a discussion phase before being categorized.  



## **:tools: Thread Lifecycle**  

- **Open Discussion** – Actively discussed, anyone can contribute.  

- **Locked** – No further replies allowed, but still visible.  

- **Closed** – Moved to “Older Posts” once categorized or archived.  



---------------------------------------------------------------------

## **:label: Tags & Their Meanings**  

*(Use these to categorize your idea—admins will adjust if needed.)*  



### **Status & Moderation Tags (Admin-Only)**  

:white_check_mark: **Open Discussion** – Idea is being actively discussed.  

:x: **Closed** – Moved, rejected, or archived.  

:arrows_counterclockwise: **Duplicate** – Similar idea exists; linked to original.  

:construction: **Under-Development** – Actively being worked on but hasn’t been categorized yet.  

:star: **Flagship Project** – High-priority or significant project.  



### **Priority Tags**  

:star: **Flagship Project** – High-priority or major undertaking. *(Admin-Only)*  

:bulb: **Conceptual** – Just an idea, not fully developed.  



### **Project Scope Tags**  

:pick: **Minecraft** – Any Minecraft-related project.  

:robot: **AI & Automation** – AI-driven tools, machine learning, or automation.  

:wrench: **Plugin Development** – Custom plugin-based projects.  

:tools: **Game Modding** – Any modifications to existing games.  

:scroll: **Scripting** – Bots, macros, or automation scripts.  

:zap: **Utility Software** – General-purpose tools that don’t fit into mods/plugins.  

:test_tube: **Experimental** – “Is this possible?” projects.  



### **Technical Tags (For Coding Projects)**  

:coffee: **Java**  

:globe_with_meridians: **JavaScript**  

:jigsaw: **JSM** (JavaScript Macro)

# **:pushpin: Additional Sections for Clarity**  



## **:round_pushpin: How Ideas Become Projects**  

### **1. Discussion Phase:**  

- Users refine and expand on the idea.  

- If interest grows, it gets flagged as **In Progress**.  



### **2. Admin Review:**  

- If the idea has enough potential, it gets a dedicated project category.  

- **Admins will create the necessary project channels and sort it under Public or Private Projects.**  



### **3. Final Thread Lock & Close:**  

- The last message in the thread will indicate where the idea has moved.  

- Example: *"Thread locked. Project moved to <#category-link>."*

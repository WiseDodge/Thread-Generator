**:pencil: Post Guidelines**



**:one: Use a Clear & Descriptive Title**  

**Bad:** “Cool idea”  

**Good:** “Minecraft Plugin: Custom Fluid Physics”  





**:two: Select Relevant Tags**  

- **Category tags** (Minecraft, AI, Plugin, etc.)  

- If unsure, use **Misc**; admins will adjust.  





**:three: Describe Your Idea in Detail**  

- What problem does it solve?

- How does it work? *(Rough overview)*  

- Challenges & Limitations?

- How complex is it? *(Simple, Moderate, Advanced?)*  





**:four: Check for Duplicates**  

- Before posting, __search the forum__ to see if a similar idea already exists.  

- If an idea is a slight variation, reply to the existing thread instead.  

- If your idea is flagged as a **Duplicate**, an admin will link you to the original discussion.  



**:five: Keep Discussion Focused**  

- Stay on-topic; brainstorm __solutions__ rather than derailing the idea.  

- Keep discussions constructive; vague “this won’t work” comments won’t help.
